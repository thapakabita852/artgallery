Julia Singh is a talented painter known for her vibrant and abstract artwork. Her use of bold colors and unique brushstrokes creates visually captivating pieces that evoke a sense of emotion and energy.

Murat Ansari is a renowned sculptor whose work primarily focuses on creating intricate sculptures using various materials such as marble, bronze, and wood. His sculptures often explore themes of human emotions and the beauty of nature.
